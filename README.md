# ClockWiz
Remake of the desk clock app from the Samsung Galaxy S3-S4
<img width="2249" height="997" alt="ClockWizzers" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f28d6d7c-b0c6-4b9e-b0e3-d6fb138960b6" />

For Android 2.3+

Tap the screen to bring up the settings, there you can change the background style or enable/disable the calendar

- Standard: The sky background, dynamically changes to night or day according to the clock (night starts at 18:00/6PM and ends at 5:00 AM)
- Like start: Your wallpaper as the background, good for live wallpapers
